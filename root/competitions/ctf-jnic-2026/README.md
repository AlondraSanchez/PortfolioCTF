# 🏆 Capture The Flag JNIC (*Jornadas Nacionales de Investigación en Ciberseguridad*)

## 📅 Context

* Duration: April 30 (20:00) - May 2 (23:59)
* Team size: Up to 3 participants (we competed as a team of 2)
* Categories: Web, Pwn, Crypto, Misc, Forensics

## 🎯 Overview

This competition involved a wide range of challenges covering multiple areas of cybersecurity, including:
- Reverse engineering
- Vulnerability exploitation
- Digital forensics
- Web application security
- Cryptography and steganography

## 🛠️ My Contribution

- Personally captured **7 out of 8 flags** entirely
- Contributed **~50%** to the remaining challenge
- Focused primarily on:
    - Web exploitation
    - Reverse engineering
    - Forensics analysis

During the competition, I was responsible for:
- Identifying attack vectors
- Developing exploitation strategies
- Analyzing challenge artifacts and extracting relevant data

## 🔥 Key Takeaways

- Learned to **prioritize challenges under time pressure** and focus on high-impact solutions
- Improved ability to **quickly identify vulnerability patterns** across different categories
- Strengthened **problem-solving in unstructured environments** (no guided paths like labs)
- Reinforced collaboration and communication in a small team setting
